Oregon Transportation Budgets

Throughout the state of Oregon, transportation infrastructure is funded through three different levels: Local (City), County, and State. This piece will provide an interactive tool that allows the Oregon Legislative Assembly explore the Local and County transportation budgets.
Use the arrow in the bottom left corner to navigate through layers, or hover to the left of this box to select a specific scene.
